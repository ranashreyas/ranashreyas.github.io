Overview

The Kinetic Mirror was a project I led since the Summer of 2020. The Mirror is an Interdiciplinary fusion between Robotics and Art. The basic concept behind the Mirror, is that it detects the form or pose of a person standing in front of it, and it will rotate all 100 individual mirrors to reflect back a pixelated image of the person in front.
